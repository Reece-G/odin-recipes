Long Island Iced Tea

Desciption

The long island iced tea... an easy cocktail to make that packs a punch while tasting great!

This cocktail consists of five different alcoholic ingredients shaken over ice, mixed with coke and topped with a slice of lemon.

Ingredients

  • 20ml Vodka
  • 20ml white rum
  • 20ml Silver tequila
  • 20ml Gin
  • 20ml Triple sec
  • 20ml simple syrup
  • 20ml lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • Coke to top
  • Garnish with lemon slice

Step-by-Step

  1. Gather together the ingredients
  2. Measure out the alcoholic ingredients into a cocktail shaker with ice
  3. Add the lemon juice and syrup to the cocktail shaker
  4. Shake the mix together
  5. Prepare a Collins glass with large ice cubes
  6. Pour the mix into the glass
  7. Top the mix with coke
  8. Mix it all together with a straw
  9. Top with a slice of lemon
  10. Enjoy!
