Cookies

Description

The cookie, a famous and world renowned treat the world over!

Made from simple ingredients the cookie is delicious, especially served warm with ice cream.

ingredients

  • 120g softened butter
  • 75g light brown sugar
  • 75g golden caster sugar
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 180g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 150g dark chocolate
