Cookies

Description

The cookie, a famous and world renowned treat the world over!

Made from simple ingredients the cookie is delicious, especially served warm with ice cream.

ingredients

  • 120g softened butter
  • 75g light brown sugar
  • 75g golden caster sugar
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 180g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 150g dark chocolate

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Heat oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4 and line two baking sheets with parchment.
  3. Cream the butter and sugars together until very light and fluffy, then beat in the egg and vanilla.
  4. Once combined, stir in the flour, bicarb, chocolate and ¼ tsp salt.
  5. Scoop 10 large tbsps of the mixture onto the trays, leaving enough space between each to allow for spreading.
  6. Bake for 10-12 mins or until firm at the edges but still soft in the middle – they will harden a little as they cool.
  7. Leave to cool on the tray for a few mins before eating warm, or transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. Will keep for three days in an airtight container.
